Welcome to HMS Reception Dashboard

Here you will find all the essential features and functionalities of a Hospital Management System (HMS) Reception Dashboard, designed to streamline patient interactions and improve efficiency at the hospital front desk.

Features:

  • Patient Registration: Capture and manage patient details for new and returning patients.
  • Doctor's Schedule: View and manage doctor availability and appointments.
  • Billing and Payments: Process payments, generate receipts, and verify insurance.
  • Patient Check-In and Check-Out: Manage arrivals, discharges, and final billing.
  • Notifications: Real-time alerts about appointments and emergencies.
  • Queue Management: Organize waiting lists and inform patients about wait times.
  • Visitor Management: Log visitor details and issue passes for those accompanying patients.
